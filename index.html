NF | Drifting

About

"Drifting is the 8th track of the Clouds (THE MIXTAPE) album released in 2022 created and produced by NF (Nathan Feuerstein) and Tommee Profitt. It is a song that highlights struggles, regrets, and the search for one's purpose in life."
